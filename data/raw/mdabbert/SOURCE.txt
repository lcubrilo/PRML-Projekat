Snapshot pinned for reproducibility
-----------------------------------
mdabbert is a "constant work in progress" and is refreshed over time. This
committed copy is the exact snapshot the project's reported results are
derived from, so they reproduce without a Kaggle download.
  - Rows:  7177 fights
  - Cols:  118
  - Range: 2010-03-21 .. 2026-03-28

Provenance
----------
Generated by the shortlikeafox/ultimate_ufc_dataset scraper; fight statistics
ultimately derive from ufcstats.com (the public successor to FightMetric).
Fields include pre-fight career aggregates, physical attributes, betting odds
(moneyline + per-method props), and the fight result (Winner, finish method).

Re-fetch (newer snapshot)
-------------------------
  python -m kaggle datasets download -d mdabbert/ultimate-ufc-dataset \
      -p data/raw/mdabbert --unzip

See docs/DATASETS.md for the full column-level inspection and leakage audit.
